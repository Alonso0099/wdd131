Costa Rica

Costa Rica is a country in Central America, bordered by Nicaragua and Panama, with coastlines on both the Pacific Ocean and the Caribbean Sea. It is internationally recognized for its protected areas and national parks, which help preserve a wide variety of wildlife and ecosystems. The country is also known for its stability and strong focus on education and environmental protection.
